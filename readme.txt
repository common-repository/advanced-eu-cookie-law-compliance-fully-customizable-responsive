== Description ==

Accordingly to the European Directive, adopted by all European Countries in May 2011, all websites based or doing business to any European Country,
are required to inform their visitors for any usage of cookies. Any visitor must be informed about how any personal information is being stored and used, 
and give consent by clicking the Accept button.

With our Advanced EU Cookie Law Plugin you can add the Cookie Law Box in minutes and fully customize it in order to match your desired look and feel.
You can control the appearance of any element:

1. Position of the Window: Header or Footer
2. Background Colors, Padding, and Font Color, Height & Width of the Cookie Window
3. Text, Background Colors, Padding, and Font Color, Border Radius & Color of the Agree Button
4. Text, Background Colors, Padding, and Font Color, Border Radius & Color of The Learn More Button
5. Selection of the Privacy Policy page

You can easily customize the appearance by playing around with all our settings, or visit our 100% Free EU Cookie Law Theme on our website!

Also, our Advanced Cookie Law will create a pseudo CSS in order to appear above your header (if placed on Header) or below your footer (if placed on footer).  Your user will not miss any information as a result of the use of the Cookie Law Window. 
The Window is 100% responsive and will appear smoothly in any device.
